# brightness and contrast


In this section, we first introduce the concept of **Contrast** and explore histogram-based contrast enhancement techniques, including **Histogram Stretching**, **Histogram Equalization**, and **CLAHE (Contrast Limited Adaptive Histogram Equalization)**. We also discuss the advantages, limitations, and applications of each method and compare their performance in different scenarios.

Next, we introduce the concept of **Brightness** and explain how it differs from contrast. We then cover common brightness adjustment techniques, such as **Linear Brightness Adjustment** and **Gamma Correction**, and examine when each method is most appropriate for enhancing images, especially those captured under low-light conditions.
